The Scoreboard
Grades reflect a weighted composite of all available sources, normalized to 100. The shaded band runs from a film's lowest score to its highest — when it stretches past 25 points we stamp it Critics Divided, which is usually your cue to see it anyway. Reddit numbers are sentiment from recent r/movies and r/boxoffice chatter: a vibe, not a verdict.
